what is 7am

dating without the mask

every dating app lets you upload your best photo from two years ago, add a filter, pick the perfect angle. you show up to the date and they don't look anything like their profile.

7am fixes that. your entire profile is three short video clips — recorded live, in the app, right now. no uploads. no edits. no second takes. you also read a random script out loud so people hear your real voice before they swipe.

it's called 7am because that's when nobody's pretending.

how it works
01

02

03

record your world

show where you are right now. your room, your street, your life. context matters.
