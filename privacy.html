privacy policy

last updated: april 2026

what we collect

when you create an account, we collect your email address, name, age, gender, and dating preference. this information is necessary to provide the service.

when you record profile clips, the video files are uploaded to our secure cloud storage. these videos are visible to other users of the app as part of your dating profile.

when you use the chat feature, your messages are stored in our database to enable conversation between matched users.

how we use your data

your profile information is used to show your profile to other users and to filter matches based on your preferences. we do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

your email address is used for account authentication and may be used to send important service-related communications.

data storage

your data is stored securely using supabase, which provides encrypted database storage and secure file hosting. video files are stored in cloud storage with access controlled by authentication policies.

your password is hashed and never stored in plain text. we cannot see your password.

your rights

you can view and edit your profile information at any time through the app's profile and settings screens.

you can delete your account at any time from the settings screen. when you delete your account, all of your data is permanently removed, including your profile, video clips, matches, messages, and authentication credentials. this action cannot be undone.

you can request a copy of your data by contacting us at the email below.

video content

all video content must be recorded live within the app. the app does not allow uploading pre-recorded content or applying digital filters. by recording clips, you consent to other users viewing your video profile.

reporting and blocking

if you report another user, we store the report details (your user id, the reported user's id, and the reason) for review. if you block a user, neither of you will see each other's profiles or be able to communicate.

third-party services

we use the following third-party services: supabase (authentication, database, file storage), expo (app development and distribution), and formspree (landing page email collection). each service has its own privacy policy.

children

7am is not intended for anyone under the age of 18. we do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. if we become aware that a user is under 18, we will delete their account immediately.

changes to this policy

we may update this privacy policy from time to time. significant changes will be communicated through the app.

contact

if you have questions about this privacy policy or your data, contact us at support@7am.app
