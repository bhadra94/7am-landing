safety standards

last updated: april 2026

our commitment to safety

7am is committed to creating a safe environment for all users. we have zero tolerance for child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) of any kind on our platform.

age requirement

7am is strictly for users aged 18 and over. we collect and verify age during account registration. any user found to be under 18 will have their account immediately terminated and all associated data permanently deleted.

prohibited content

the following content is strictly prohibited on 7am and will result in immediate account termination and reporting to relevant authorities: any content depicting, promoting, or facilitating child sexual abuse or exploitation; any content involving minors in sexual or suggestive contexts; any attempt to solicit, groom, or exploit minors; and any content that sexualizes minors in any way.

detection and prevention

all video content on 7am is recorded live within the app. we do not allow pre-recorded uploads, which reduces the risk of prohibited content being distributed. users can report any content or behavior that violates our safety standards using the in-app reporting feature.

reporting and enforcement

7am provides in-app tools for users to report concerning content or behavior. all reports are reviewed and acted upon. when we identify content that constitutes child sexual abuse material (CSAM), we report it to the national center for missing and exploited children (NCMEC) and relevant law enforcement authorities as required by law.

users who violate our safety standards are permanently banned from the platform. we cooperate fully with law enforcement investigations related to child safety.

user responsibilities

all users are required to comply with our safety standards. if you encounter any content or behavior that may involve the exploitation of minors, please report it immediately using the in-app report feature or by contacting us directly.
